What is Launchpad Kerala?

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is one of the most prestigious events organized by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. This event is dedicated to creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates by connecting them with innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. The aim is to foster meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Launchpad Kerala Highlights

Unique Assessment Approach

Launchpad Kerala 2024 features a standout assessment process that includes a rigorous technical challenge and online tests. This thorough evaluation of technical and non-technical skills ensures that only the best candidates proceed to interviews, providing unparalleled opportunities for both candidates and companies.

Flexible Interview Locations

Shortlisted candidates can opt for interviews in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode. This flexibility streamlines the hiring process for companies and maximizes participation from top talent across the state.

Comprehensive Talent Pool

The event boasts a diverse talent pool sourced from across Kerala, catering to various skill sets and experience levels. This provides companies with access to top-tier candidates for a multitude of roles.

Strategic Partnerships

The collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn enhances the impact of Launchpad Kerala 2024. This partnership ensures candidates undergo a thorough assessment, while companies gain access to top talent and increased brand visibility.

Benefits for Recruiters

Enhanced Brand Visibility

Recruiters can stand out among competitors by showcasing their brand's ethos and career opportunities to top talent, solidifying their position as an employer of choice in the industry.

Cost-Efficient Recruitment Solutions

By participating in Launchpad Kerala, companies can slash recruitment costs and streamline their hiring process, ensuring optimal resource allocation while accessing the best talent Kerala has to offer.

Access to Elite Talent

Recruiters will have the opportunity to tap into a diverse pool of top-tier professionals, graduates, and emerging stars. This equips organizations with the skills and expertise needed for sustainable growth and innovation.

Talent Pipeline Enrichment

Companies can cultivate a future-ready workforce by nurturing relationships with promising candidates. Offering internships and fostering professional development initiatives ensures a continuous flow of skilled talent ready to drive the company's success.

Benefits for Students

Networking Opportunities

Students can network with professionals and representatives from various innovative companies. This allows them to establish valuable connections for potential internships, co-op opportunities, and future employment.

Access to Industry Insights

Attending the job fair gives students exposure to the latest trends, technologies, and challenges in their field. They can gain insights into industry best practices, upcoming projects, and the skills in demand by speaking directly with industry professionals.

Exposure to Diverse Career Paths

Launchpad Kerala features a wide range of companies across different sectors and industries, exposing students to diverse career paths they may not have considered before. This helps them explore various options and find the right fit for their interests and skills.

Personal Development

Participating in Launchpad Kerala 2024 offers students a transformative experience, fostering personal growth and development. Through the assessment process, students push their limits, embrace new opportunities, and cultivate resilience, adaptability, and self-confidence.
